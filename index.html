WELCOME TO DUBLINORAMA!

Welcome to Dublinorama, the home of live music in Dublin.

Dublinorama opened its doors in 2021 to revive the local live music scene in Dublin after the global pandemic. The venue hosts live concerts and other music events on a weekly basis. Dublinorama has the capacity to host 3000 fellow music lovers. Find live music for tonight or check out upcoming gigs in the 'Events' section of the website or go to the gallery section to see some amazing pictures!
